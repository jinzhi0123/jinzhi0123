英语四级长阅读做题方法与技巧

发表于2021-11-18|更新于2021-12-21|经验分享
|字数总计:2.2k|阅读时长:8分钟|阅读量:

前排提醒

  1. 题目与段落不是一一对应关系，不是每一个段落都会被选。
  2. 同一个段落可能是多个问题的答案，即一个段落可能会被选多次。

做题顺序

  1. 先看题目，再读文章！把段落拎出来去和10个问题对照！不要盯着一个问题去和一个一个段落对。
  2. 拿到一个题目，勾画出所有的细节，包括但不限于人物（物体、事情）、时间、地点以及数词、介词等限制词。
  3. 总共有十个题目，先勾画5个题目，另外5个先不要看，把勾画出来的读1遍，争取把这5个题目的细节记住。
  4. 然后对剩下的5个题目进行同样的操作，勾画，读1遍。
  5. 接着把这10个题目勾画出来的细节再看一遍，为的就是要对这10个题目有印象。
  6. 读的时候可以试着翻译/概括，加深记忆。当然时间不够的话就不要这样做了。
  7. 接着就是读文章了，从第一个段落开始，一个段落一个段落来，边读边勾画关键词（着重注意主客体，即人物、物体、事情），时间地点数词等限制词。把这些关键词与题目中勾画的关键词做比对。

解题技巧

注意这些技巧不是独立的，而是相辅相成的、综合在一起的，使用的时候注意结合。

注意主客体（人物、物体、事情）

通过主客体，我们可以快速了解段落讲的是哪个范畴，能够帮助我们快速定位。

当在问题中勾画的主客体在某个段落中全都出现时，需要高度注意，两者很可能是相匹配的。

同样，当问题中勾画的主客体的某一个或更多在某个段落中完全没有出现（替换词也没有），也需要高度注意，两者可能不匹配。而且很大概论确实不匹配，因为段落中缺少了必要的元素。

例1：

The number of people experiencing depression shows a sharp increase in the first year of high school.

In addition，studies find the first year of high school typically shows one of the greatest increases in depression of any year over the lifespan. Researchers think that one explanation is that ties to friends are broken while academic demands are rising. Furthermore, most adult cases of clinical depression first emerge in adolescence（青春期）.The World Health Organization reports that depression has the greatest burden of disease worldwide，in terms of the total cost of treatment and the loss of productivity.

拿到题目，我们把the number of people、depression、a sharp increase、the first year of high school这几个主体以及动词show这个动词勾画出来。

当然你把 in the first year of high school作为时间来看也没有任何问题。

我们可以看到这几个主体与段落完全匹配，快速浏览段落发现该段落确实在说这个事情，完全OK！

例2：

Researchers would like to explore further how parents and schools can help ninth graders by changing their mindset.

These findings lead to several possibilities that we are investigating further. First, we are working to replicate（复制）these findings in more diverse school communities. We want to know in which types of schools and for which kinds of students these growth mindset ideas help young people adapt to the challenges of high school. We also hope to learn how teachers, parents or school counselors can help students keep their ongoing academic or social difficulties in perspective. We wonder what would happen if schools helped to make beliefs about the potential for change and improvement a larger feature of the overall school culture, especially for students starting the ninth grade.

拿到题目，我们把parents and schools、ninth garders、mindset以及explore、help、change这几个动词圈出来。

同时would like和further这个副词也可以勾画出来辅助使用。

可以看到这几个主体在题目中都有对应的词存在（用粗体标出）。

同时explore further与investigate further对应， want to know、hope to learn、wonder与would like to explore意思对应，完全OK！

注意数词

要注意表示数量、范围的词，特别是明确的数字。这是很强的限制词，数字都对不上，能匹配吗？

但是从另一个范围来讲，连数字都对上了，两者很大概论就是匹配的。

如果问题中出现了数字，这是需要高度注意的词，画出来！如果文章里没有对应的数词，很有可能两者并不匹配！

问题：2021.6（1）
According to one study, each high school graduate contributes at least 500,000 dollars to the local economy.

例1：

段落：
In the new global economy, students who fail to finish the ninth grade with passing grades in college preparatory coursework are very unlikely to graduate on time and go on to get jobs. One study has calculated that the lifetime benefit to the local economy for a single additional student who completes high school is half a million dollars or more. This is based on higher earnings and avoided costs in health care, crime, welfare dependence and other things.

这里的at least 500,000和half a million高度符合，

同时high school graduate和students who completes high school相对应，

以及local economy也在段落中提及，两个主体完全对应，基础上就OK了。

注意时间和地点

时间和地点同样是限制性的词，这必须是匹配的，跟上面的数词差不多。

动词、副词、形容词、连词作为辅助

因为很多事情动词、副词、形容词对问题的判断不是决定性的，我们通过可以拿来做辅助判断。

due to, because, only, all

我们拿一个上面的例子：

Researchers would like to explore further how parents and schools can help ninth graders by changing their mindset.

These findings lead to several possibilities that we are investigating further. First, we are working to replicate（复制）these findings in more diverse school communities. We want to know in which types of schools and for which kinds of students these growth mindset ideas help young people adapt to the challenges of high school. We also hope to learn how teachers, parents or school counselors can help students keep their ongoing academic or social difficulties in perspective. We wonder what would happen if schools helped to make beliefs about the potential for change and improvement a larger feature of the overall school culture, especially for students starting the ninth grade.

这里的would like to explore further就是很好的辅助判断词。

问题标注与干扰排除

有的时候，题目中有多个主语，但是并不能全部匹配。比如说，有三个主语，其中两个跟文章段落匹配，还有一个主语在该段落中找不到了。或者出现那个似匹配非匹配或者说大意似对非对的情况，让你犹豫不决。

你可以先把这个问题放一下，把这个段落的选项先填上去，并在这个段落和题目的序号上都做个标记，提醒你这个不确定。然后接着往下做。

因为往往可能这个选项是干扰项，真正的答案在下面的段落。同时先放着往下做，可以先把确定的段落选掉排除，缩小排查的范围。

如果到最后实在不行了，或者说时间不够了，你就以它为答案好了。

这里举一个上面的例子：

Researchers would like to explore further how parents and schools can help ninth graders by changing their mindset.

（B）This should matter a great deal to parents, teachers and policymakers. Ultimately it should matter to the students themselves and society at large，because students’ experience of transitioning（过渡）to the ninth grade can have long-term consequences not only for the students themselves but for their home communities. We make these observations as research psychologists who have studied how schools and families can help young people thrive.

（J）These findings lead to several possibilities that we are investigating further. First, we are working to replicate（复制）these findings in more diverse school communities. We want to know in which types of schools and for which kinds of students these growth mindset ideas help young people adapt to the challenges of high school. We also hope to learn how teachers, parents or school counselors can help students keep their ongoing academic or social difficulties in perspective. We wonder what would happen if schools helped to make beliefs about the potential for change and improvement a larger feature of the overall school culture, especially for students starting the ninth grade.

可以看到，B在J的前面，所以我们是先处理B段落的。

题目中的parents and schools、ninth graders这两个主语在段落B中都出现了，动词help也有。

但是mindset没有出现，似乎也没有替换词，这是就需要当心了。我们细读B段落，发现该段落没有体现出would like to explore、change their mindest的意思，似乎不怎么相符。

这是就可以先把B填上去，然后把题目和段落的序号都做上记号，接着往下做，直到最后一段J段，就真相大白了。

无效段落？

如果你目前在看的那个段落找不到对应的问题，你可以先放一下，给序号做个标记（记得与之前的标记区分）。过完整片文章后，针对剩下的问题再做选择。

其他

对于题目中According to ...、Reachers found that这种词（经常出现）一般来说没什么用（因为很多时候文章里面到处都是“…的研究表明”，都是调查结果），但在有的时候可以也可以帮到你。

如果一个段落整段都没有出现“研究表明”这种意思，很有可能与题目是不匹配的。
